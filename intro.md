# Welcome to The university library's data lab Python-toolbox course site

[The university library's Data Lab Upcoming Events](https://kubkalender.kb.dk/calendar/datalab?cid=6416&t=d&d=0000-00-00&cal=6416&inc=0)

This site is a collection of notebooks that have been made for workshops and consultations at the University of Copenhagen, Faculty of Humanities. It is not intended as a single course.

The concept of the site is to provide an overview of Python notebooks that can act as the foundation for workshops.

On the site you can at the moment find examples of working with Chinese text (preprocessing with jieba and harvesting articles from People's Daily), harvesting text from the web, term weighting with TF-IDF, and analysing digitized books from The Royal Danish Library (regex cleaning, collocation, lemmatization and POS tagging). There are also notebooks on building a text preprocessing pipeline, identifying textual changes between editions, and not least working with Telegram chat exports, for which there is growing interest.

Based on individual needs, I can select relevant notebooks and string them together to form a tailored workshop. Send an email to Lars Kjær (lakj/-/at/-/kb.dk).

The site is under development and it is not a goal to be finished. I will continue to add new content.
